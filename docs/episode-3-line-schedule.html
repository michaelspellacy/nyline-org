NYLine III 2005 Line Schedule for Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith

A minimum of 60 points is required to earn a ticket for the opening-night screening.

If you are traveling from outside the tri-state area, you automatically qualify for the following point bonuses:

Outside tri-state region: 5 points
Central: 10 points
Mountain: 15 points
Pacific: 20 points
International: 60 points
