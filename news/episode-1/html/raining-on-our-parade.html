The rainy conditions have not hampered the festivities of a few hearty souls who are waiting out the store by practicing for this evenings Yomega yo-yo competition.

However, the inclement weather has prevented the reestablishing of electricity and communications from Echo Base.

Please stay tuned as we will be back online and on line when the ground dries.

Over $200.00 has been raised by the standees for the Starlight Children's Foundation collecting money from passers by in the first two days.
