--- id: television-news-coverage-scheduled-for-sunday section: news theme: episode-1 ---

ABC and NBC will be vying for the NYLine's attention as crews from ABC-7 and "Sunday Today" visit the line Sunday morning.

Any line members in metro area are urged to show up at the Ziegfeld theatre by 7:30 a.m. This is an excellent opportunity to raise awareness for the Starlight Children's Foundation, and our presence will be more impressive if we have a visible crowd gathered.

If you can't make it to the Ziegfeld, be sure to set your VCRs. ABC-7 will be broadcasting from the line during its weather segments throughout the morning. Check your local listings for NBC's "Sunday Today".
