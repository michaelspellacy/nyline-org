FYI, The Phantom Menace will only play in 6 theaters in Manhattan, including of course the Ziegfeld, and the Clearview First & 62nd St. Cinema, United Artists' Union Square 14, the Olympia Twin on 103rd St. and Broadway, the Murray Hill and the newly refurbished 86th Street.

Loews will not play the picture on any of its screens in Manhattan, since they didn't manage to reach an agreement on rental terms with Fox.
