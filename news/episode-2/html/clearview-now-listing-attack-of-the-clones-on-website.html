Clearview Cinemas has confirmed its special digital presentation of "Star Wars Episode II: Attack of the Clones" at the Ziegfeld Theater. Other New York City Clearview theaters booked to show "Attack of the Clones" include the Chelsea Cinemas, the Park and 86th Street Cinemas and the Olympia Cinemas.

The date for advance ticket sales has not yet been announced. For more information, see the Clearview web site.
