The following is a chart of all shifts that will be available once the sign up form is live on this site in a few short days.

Keep in mind that a total of 45 shift points, a money order for $15.00 and a signed waiver (which you will be able to print out) is all that are required to guarantee you the best seat in New York City for the midnight screening of 'Episode II'.

Preferred seating arrangements and prizes will be given to those with the highest shift points and those who raise the most money for Starlight.

Doing your part to help raise money for The Starlight Children's Foundation is strongly encouraged of all who are participating. Remember, the main reasons we are lining up early is to help those children who are less fortunate then ourselves. If you know anyone who would like to sponsor you, the appropriate form can be downloaded here.

*Note: The NYLine Sponsor Form requires Adobe Acrobat Reader which can be downloaded for FREE by clicking here.

Also, following the rules of the line up is of the utmost importance. Be sure to read the general rules for signing up by clicking here.

Our last mandatory requirment for lining up is that everyone have a great time!

Now, start planning those days off from work or school...
