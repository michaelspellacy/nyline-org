Big Apple Comic Con and SWNYC Proudly Present...

MICHAEL SHEARD (ADMIRAL OZZEL) FIRST TIME IN NYC!!!!

The Big Apple Comic Con Promoters Mike and John have teamed up with Alan and Christina of SWNYC to bring the talented British actor Michael Sheard to the New York area! Michael has appeared in such notable films and television shows as Doctor Who, Indiana Jones and the Temple Of Doom and, of course, STAR WARS: THE EMPIRE STRIKES BACK.

Michael is very excited to be making his first Tri-State area appearance ever at the Big Apple Comic Con and looks forward to meeting fans Fri, March 29th and Sat March 30th.

Michael will be available for autographs all day, both days, at the show. So, stop by and say hello to the folks from SWNYC and Michael. Admission is only $6.00! Big Apple Comic Con is your one stop source for all your collectible needs. Check out their website here for more details.

Plus, Michael has agreed to join the fans for a sit down dinner to help raise money to bring kids to see the Magic of the Myth exhibit at the Brooklyn Museum of Art. Details to follow regarding the charity dinner. SWNYC standing rule is absolutely NO Autographs at the dinner are permitted! (We want to let Michael enjoy his meal and his time with the fans.)
