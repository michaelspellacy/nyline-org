Lucasfilm was kind enough to donate some wonderful items to eBay's "Auction for America." Included are a Lightsaber used by Ray Park in filming Episode I, a Stormtrooper helmet used in ESB and ROTJ, a Vader helmet signed by the boss man himself and lots of other goodies! My fav is the R2D2 & C3PO model, I love the detail!

Check it out and bust out those credit cards! Too bad I'm one broke bloke...
