MEPA? What the heck is MEPA?

Well my friends, MEPA stands for Mos Eisely Port Authority (a cool name if I do say so myself) and it is the new name of the Official New York FanForce Chapter.

SWNYC, LLC is now a private corporation and has no affiliation with Star Wars, TheForce.Net, or LucasFilm. SWNYC now stands for Sci-Fi World New York City.

So now you know...and knowing is half the battle - d'oh! I couldn't help myself!
