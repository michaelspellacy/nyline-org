SWNYC invites everyone to join them in their line up for the press viewing of The Magic of Myth at the Brooklyn Museum of Art on Thursday, April 4th at 10:00 AM. For those interested in attending you must arrive before 10:00 AM to view the exhibit at 1:00 PM. Free snacks will be served while we wait.

Everyone is encouraged to wear a Star Wars Costume. Please see the museums weapon restrictions posted here. The Empire City Garrison of the 501st legion will be in attendance as well.

Please let us know if you will be attending or if you have any questions contact us by e- mailing SWNYC Staff.
