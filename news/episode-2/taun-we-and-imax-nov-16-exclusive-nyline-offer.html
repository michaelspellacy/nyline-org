Haven't had a chance to see "Attack of the Clones" on the really big screen? NYLine members who attend a screening Saturday Nov. 16 at the Sony Lincoln Square can add to the "Star Wars" excitement by meeting Rena Owen, the actress who played Taun We in Episode II, at Ollie's, 1991 Broadway.

Ms. Owen, who also appeared in "A.I.," "Once Were Warriors" and the current season of TV's "Angel," will be meeting fans and selling autographed "Star Wars" photographs, action figures and Episode II DVDs from 11 a.m. to 10 p.m.

As a special bonus exclusive to NYLine members, Ms. Owen will give a $5 discount on any single autographed item to everybody who proudly shows their NYLine badge! For example, the Episode II DVD, which sells for $30, would be $25 for any NYLine member presenting a badge. For more information, click here.
