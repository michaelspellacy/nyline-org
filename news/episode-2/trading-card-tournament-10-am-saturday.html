--- members: 28 published: "2002-05-10 19:52:23" publisher: Steve section: news title: Trading Card Tournament - 10 a.m. Saturday!! theme: episode-2 ---

Forbidden Planet will be hosting a tournament with the new Wizards of the Coast "Star Wars" trading card game at 10 a.m. Saturday.

The first 36 NYLine members to sign up will receive a free Light Side or Dark Side starter deck and one booster pack. The tournament will be single- elimination, and the grand prize winner will receive "The Art of Episode II: Attack of the Clones," a hardcover book featuring concept art and the script for the movie that has us all lining up!

To sign up, call the NYLine phone at the Ziegfeld (212) 664-9764. The first people to sign up will be able to requst whether they're on the light or the dark side until all the decks for one side are given away.
