--- members: 23 published: "2002-05-10 17:18:16" publisher: Dave section: news title: "You Are Invited: Saturday, May 11" theme: episode-2 ---

You are cordially invited to attend a Star Wars Wedding Ceremony on Saturday, May 11, 2002 at 2:00pm, "on the line" in front of the Ziegfeld Theater, 141 West 54th Street in Manhattan.

Diana C. and Brian T. will be declaring their union, having been brought together by the magic of Star Wars shortly after the premiere of Episode I in 1999. Because Star Wars has been such an important influence on both of their lives, they are looking forward to sharing their excitement with friends and fans as they wait for the premiere of Episode II in front or the Zig.

Guests are encouraged to come in the costume of their favorite Star Wars character. Brian and Diana hope to see you there!

Update: The Connan O'Brian show will be present for the event.
