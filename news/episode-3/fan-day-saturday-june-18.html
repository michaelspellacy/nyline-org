In both 1999 and 2002, the one-month anniversary of the opening of a new "Star Wars" movie was celebrated as "Fan Day" - a chance for NYLine members to get together, hang out on 54th Street (and elsewhere) and take in one more screening at the Ziegfeld.

As we believe in honoring tradition, plans are starting to take shape for a Fan Day event befitting NYLine III. This year's event will take place a day early so as not to conflict with (I'm Your) Father's Day. If you'd like to be a part of the planning process or Fan Day itself, please head over to this thread on the NYLine message boards.

See you June 18!
