After Saturday, you'll have your Episode III toys, but CIII and NYLine are still weeks away. How do you keep the "Star Wars" momentum rolling? Celebrate "Star Wars" and the upcoming release of Episode III with Monkeys In The Atrium, NYC's newest comedy improv & sketch group! They have a brand new show featuring written sketches and improv skits, all based on the "Star Wars" characters and mythology, plus they'll be giving away free movie tickets and X-Box video games! Visit the Monkeys in the Atrium website, where you can also make reservations (suggested but not required).

MITA: The Star Wars Show

Thursday, April 7, 8 p.m.

Charley O's Bar & Grill - 713 8th Ave. @ 45th Street

$5 cover + $10 food/drink minimum per person; come from drinks, snacks, or dinner!
