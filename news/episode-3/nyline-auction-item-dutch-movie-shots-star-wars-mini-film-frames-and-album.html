NYLine Auction Item: Dutch Movie Shots Star Wars Mini Film Frames and Album

Posted By Amit on 4/12/2005 1:46:22 AM EST
NUMBER OF PEOPLE CURRENTLY IN LINE: 0

The following item comes from a place that's a few more than 12 parsecs outside of the Rishi maze to most of us - The Netherlands, home of our dedicated line member and friend, Bart Barenbrug.  He has donated this rare and unique gift specifically for our Starlight Starbright Children's Foundation eBay auction.

 

            

An unusual item for any real Star Wars fan, this is a collection of 50 "movie shots" cards made of film frames from Star Wars, The Empire Strikes Back and Return of the Jedi. Each frame measures about 2 inches wide and 1.5 inches high.  These cards were found in bags of Smiths' potato chips in Belgium and the Netherlands in 1997 during the theatrical run of the Special Edition. The collection is complete in its original binder, including 25 pages with drawings and photos from all three movies.
