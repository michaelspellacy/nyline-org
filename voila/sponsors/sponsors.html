RED CONNECT (PRISM CSI)

The onsite high-speed ADSL connectivity that makes the Live Web Cam possible is brought to Voila.com by RED (SM), the new service from Prism Communication Services, Inc.

Based on Digital Subscriber Line (DSL) technology, RED (SM), enables users to achieve downloads of up to 1 Megabit per second (Mbps), which is nearly 17 times faster than 56Kbps and eight times faster than ISDN.

A wholly owned subsidiary of Comdisco, Inc. (NYSE: CDO), Prism Communication Services,Inc. is a next-generation advanced communications provider based in New York City.

Voila.com wishes to express its gratitude to Prism CSI for their dedication to making the live event work.

MBS PRODUCTIONS

MBS Production is providing the editing capabilities for the "NY LINE TV" event.

MBS Productions was founded in the fall of 1996 by Iann Robinson, Josh Diamond, and Jason Diamond to answer the call of every band who wanted to do a video for themselves but didn't have tons of money to do it.

"We decided to create a company that could produce, direct, shoot and edit music videos at reasonable prices for Underground bands and their labels. We pooled our knowledge of Avid Media Composer, Web Site Design and general movie making know-how to create a company that would be different from the others. We are not opposed to making a huge budget video but we also enjoy making smaller videos for smaller bands.

As we approach the 21st century we are slowly expanding. Our website knowledge is growing, our videos are being seen all over the world and we're even now trying to get our own feature film project off the ground. Even though we love doing independent videos and features, we're not opposed to doing high budget projects. We want to be involved in anything we can in the creative process of videos, films, websites and so forth. No job is too big, no fee is too big and that's how we have always looked at it.

We got involved with the NYLine Countdown team to utilize our combined skills and our love for Star Wars to benefit the Starlight Foundation and make this entire event a memorable one. Please visit our website at www.mbsproductions.com.

STARLIGHT FOUNDATION

NYLine Countdown will be utilizing the amazing, once-in-a lifetime opportunity surrounding the release of Episode I to raise money for the Starlight Children's Foundation NY*NJ*CT.

The Starlight Children's Foundation NY*NJ*CT brightens the lives of seriously ill children by granting wishes and providing a variety of entertainment and funding to hospital pediatric wards. The NY*NJ*CT chapter serves approximately 16,000 children and their families each month. Since its inception in 1985, Starlight NY*NJ*CT has granted 3,500 wishes.

NYLine Countdown will fundraise for Starlight through individual and corporate sponsorship for each hour NYLine fans spend in line; the auctioning of NYLine T-shirts; and the collection of donations on the NYLine and within the Ziegfeld Theater. Also a group of volunteers from NYLine Countdown will entertain children at an area hospital as part of Starlight's Hospital Party and Clown Program.

Please visit the Starlight Foundation web site at www.starlight.org.
